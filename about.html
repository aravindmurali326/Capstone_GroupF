About Paaj Fitness

Proudly Canadian? So are we! Paaj Fitness opened its first location in Mississauga on a bright and sunny Friday
in April 2011 with the goal of assisting Canadians in getting fit at a low cost.
Since that wonderful day, we've continued to grow rapidly, opening more than 100 locations across Canada. 
We have a gym close to you whether you're exploring Peggy and her cove in Nova Scotia or skiing the majestic mountains in British Columbia.

We want you to feel at ease in our welcoming, no-judgment gyms. Paaj Fitness has something for everyone, whether you want to get in shape,
make new friends, or simply unwind. We may lack the glitz and glam of a boutique gym, but your wallet will thank you!
